Google Workspace 09: Calendar

Calendar configuration, meeting rooms and scheduling resources.

Context: This Google Workspace lab extends the same SaaS administration environment across identity, access, collaboration and security controls.

09. Calendar

Overview

Configured Google Calendar for organisational scheduling, collaboration and meeting-room resource management.

The focus is on:

  • Efficient meeting scheduling

  • Centralised resource booking

  • Preventing scheduling conflicts

  • Supporting hybrid collaboration

Resource Management (Meeting Rooms)

A structured resource management system was implemented to manage shared meeting spaces.

This allows administrators to:

  • Define buildings and locations

  • Create bookable meeting rooms

  • Centrally manage organisational resources

Creating a Resource (Meeting Room)

A meeting room resource was created and configured.

Configuration includes:

  • Resource name: Meeting Room 1

  • Building: Office

  • Floor: 1

  • Capacity: 10 people

  • Description: Main office meeting room for team bookings

This ensures:

  • Clear identification of resources

  • Accurate capacity planning

  • Improved booking experience

Resource Assignment and Structure

The resource was linked to a building and made available to users.

This provides:

  • Organised resource hierarchy

  • Easy discovery when booking meetings

  • Centralised control over availability

Booking a Resource

A calendar event was created and a meeting room resource was assigned.

This enables:

  • Automatic reservation of the room

  • Visibility of availability

  • Prevention of double bookings

Meeting Creation with Google Meet

A meeting was created including both a room and a Google Meet link.

This configuration provides:

  • Hybrid meeting capability (in-person + remote)

  • Automatic Meet link generation

  • Integration between Calendar and Meet

Design Approach

The configuration follows:

  • Centralised resource management

  • Structured building and room hierarchy

  • Integration between Calendar and Meet

  • Support for hybrid working environments

Summary

Evidence covered:

  • Creation and management of calendar resources

  • Implementation of meeting room booking

  • Integration with Google Meet

  • Prevention of scheduling conflicts

These configurations enable efficient and scalable scheduling within the organisation.
